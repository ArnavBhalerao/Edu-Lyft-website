How Edu-Lyft Works

1. Sign Up

Create an account using your phone number and verify your identity.

2. Find a Ride

Enter pickup and destination within your campus or nearby areas.

3. Match with Students

The system connects you with verified students traveling the same route.

4. Ride Together

Share rides, split costs, and travel safely with trusted peers.
