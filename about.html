About Edu-Lyft

Edu-Lyft is a campus ride-sharing platform designed to make daily commuting safer and more affordable for college students.

The platform connects verified students traveling similar routes, helping reduce transportation costs while creating a trusted community for campus mobility.

Edu-Lyft aims to improve student transportation by combining technology, identity verification, and community-driven ride sharing.
