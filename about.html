About Edu-Lyft

Edu-Lyft is a campus ride-sharing platform designed specifically for college students. Our goal is to make daily commuting safer, cheaper, and more convenient.

Our Mission

We aim to create a trusted ride-sharing network where students travel together, reduce transportation costs, and build a safer community mobility system.

Why Edu-Lyft?

  • Student-only ride sharing
  • Affordable cost sharing
  • Verified identity system
  • Safer campus transportation
Edu-Lyft combines technology and community trust to build the future of student transportation.
