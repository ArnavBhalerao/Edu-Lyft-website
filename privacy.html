EDU-LYFT Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11 February 2026

Information We Collect

  • Full Name
  • Email Address (via Google Sign-In)

  • Date of Birth
  • Gender

We do NOT store Aadhaar numbers. Verification is processed securely through an API.

How We Use Your Information

  • Secure user authentication
  • Account verification
  • Improving platform performance
  • Preventing fraud

Data Storage & Security

  • Firebase Authentication security
  • Encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
Edu-Lyft never sells or trades personal information to third parties.
